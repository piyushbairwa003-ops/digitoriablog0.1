Company Story

In 2017, Ajmer had no world-class digital marketing agency. Thousands of businesses were invisible online. One person saw that gap — and instead of waiting for someone else to fix it, he built the solution himself. This is the story of Digitoria — and why Rajasthan has never been the same since.

Ch. 01

2017 — When It Was Just a Dream

Before there was an office, before there were clients, before there was even a logo — there was one dangerous question: "What if Ajmer had a world-class digital marketing agency?"

The Gap Kamal Saw in 2017
Most businesses ran only print ads & hoardings — with zero digital presence or strategy Online visibility was an afterthought, not a priority
Social media was treated as a hobby — not a revenue-generating channel Nobody was converting followers into customers
SEO was a buzzword that very few in Ajmer actually understood — or executed Ranking on Google was a mystery to most local businesses
Ajmer had talent, ambition, and incredible businesses — but no one was telling their story online The gap between quality and visibility was enormous

Kamal Vaswani didn't just see that gap. He made a decision that would change everything — to build the agency Ajmer desperately needed. One that would compete not just locally, but nationally. That decision became Digitoria.

Ch. 02

June 25, 2017 — The Day Digitoria Was Born

Mark the date. June 25, 2017. Not with a grand launch event. Not with a boardroom full of investors. But with the quiet, fierce conviction of one person who believed Ajmer deserved better.

Did You Know?

Digitoria started with 0 clients, 0 team members, and no office — just one belief: that Ajmer's best businesses deserved to be found online. Within 12 months, that belief had grown into 100+ satisfied clients.

That belief became the foundation everything else was built on. It became the reason 100+ businesses would eventually trust their SEO, Meta Ads, Google Ads, and website development to this one agency from Chandra Vardai Nagar, Ajmer.

That invisibility is a missed opportunity — one competitors are quietly exploiting every single day.
